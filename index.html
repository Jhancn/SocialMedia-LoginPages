Exploring Social Media

Types of Social Media Platforms

  • Social Networking

    Platforms like Facebook and LinkedIn facilitate connections and networking.

  • Photo and Video Sharing

    Instagram and TikTok focus on visual content sharing.

  • Microblogging

    Twitter allows users to share short updates and news.

  • Professional Networking

    LinkedIn helps professionals expand their networks and careers.
